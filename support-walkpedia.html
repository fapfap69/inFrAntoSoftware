Walkpedia uses your device's GPS to detect when you change streets. When it identifies a new street, it automatically searches for historical information about the person or place the street is named after and reads it aloud using text-to-speech.

üìç Why isn't Walkpedia detecting street changes?

Make sure you have:

  • Granted location permission to Walkpedia
  • Enabled location services in iOS Settings
  • Good GPS signal (try moving outdoors)
  • Started the tracking by pressing the "Start" button

üîá Why can't I hear the text-to-speech?

Check that:

  • Text-to-speech is enabled in Walkpedia Settings
  • Your device volume is turned up
  • Silent mode is not enabled
  • No other audio apps are playing

üåç Does Walkpedia work in my city?

Walkpedia works worldwide! However, the quality of information depends on Wikipedia coverage for your area. It works best in cities with rich historical documentation.

üîã Does Walkpedia drain my battery?

Walkpedia is optimized for battery efficiency. It uses significant location changes detection and configurable search intervals to minimize battery usage while maintaining functionality.

‚öôÔ∏è How do I customize the app?

Tap the gear icon to access Settings where you can:

  • Adjust text length (100-500 characters)
  • Set number of sentences (1-5)
  • Change search frequency (30-300 seconds)
  • Select language and enable/disable text-to-speech

üì± Why does the app stop when my screen locks?

Make sure you've granted "Always" location permission (not just "While Using App"). Walkpedia is designed to work in background, but iOS requires explicit permission for background location access.

Troubleshooting

üîÑ App not responding or crashing

Try these steps:

  1. Force close the app and reopen it
  2. Restart your iPhone
  3. Check for app updates in the App Store
  4. Ensure you have iOS 14.0 or later

üì∂ No information found for streets

This can happen when:

  • The street name is very local or recent
  • Wikipedia doesn't have information about the namesake
  • You're in an area with limited internet connectivity

Walkpedia will try multiple sources including Google AI as a fallback.

System Requirements

  • iOS 14.0 or later
  • iPhone or iPad with GPS capability
  • Internet connection for fetching information
  • Location services enabled
